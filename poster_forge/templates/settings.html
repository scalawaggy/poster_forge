⚠️ DANGER ZONE: ImageMaid Cleanup

                

                    This tool integrates the official Kometa-Team ImageMaid script to physically scan and delete orphaned custom posters from your live Plex Database and Metadata folder.
                

                

                    CRITICAL DOCKER REQUIREMENT: You MUST have your Plex Media Server configuration directory mounted as a volume in Poster Forge's docker-compose.yml (e.g. - /path/to/Plex Media Server:/plex:rw). Without this, the cleanup will fail.


                    DATABASE RISK: While highly vetted by the community, providing 3rd-party write-access to your live Plex SQLite Database carries an inherent risk of corruption if Plex is simultaneously running internal DB maintenance. Use entirely at your own risk. Routine backups of your Plex database are strongly recommended before running.
